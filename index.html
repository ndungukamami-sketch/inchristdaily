In Christ Daily

Privacy Policy

Effective 15 July 2026

In Christ Daily is a daily scripture confession app published by BHR AI. We built this app to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used. By using In Christ Daily, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we do not collect

In Christ Daily does not require you to create an account. We do not ask for your name, your email address, or your contacts. We do not operate our own servers that store your personal information, and we do not run our own analytics or tracking.

Information stored on your device

Your saved favorites, your daily reading progress, and your reminder time preferences are stored locally on your device using standard app storage. This information stays on your phone. It is not sent to us, and it is removed when you uninstall the app.

Notifications

The app schedules your daily confession reminder locally on your device. You choose the time in Settings, and you can turn reminders off at any time, either in the app or in your device settings. These reminders are generated on your phone and do not involve any external server.

Advertising

The free version of In Christ Daily displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve these ads, Google may collect and process certain information, such as your device advertising identifier and general information about how you use the app. By default the app requests non-personalized ads. Ad content is limited to a family friendly rating. You can read how Google uses information from apps that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Purchases

If you choose to buy the premium upgrade, the transaction is handled by Google Play Billing and processed through our purchase provider, RevenueCat. We receive a record that a purchase was made so the app can unlock premium features for you. We never receive or store your card number or payment details.

Third party services

In Christ Daily relies on the following third party services, each of which maintains its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob and Google Play, covered by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat, covered by the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

In Christ Daily is not directed to children under the age of 13. We keep ad content restricted to a family friendly rating, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through one of the third party services above, please contact us and we will help you address it with the relevant provider.

Data security

Because the app does not collect or transmit your personal information to us, there is no personal account data on our side that could be exposed. The third party providers listed above maintain their own security practices for the limited data they handle.

Your choices

You can remove all information stored on your device by uninstalling the app. You can control ad personalization and reset your advertising identifier in your device settings under the Google section. You can turn off reminders at any time in the app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date shown at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
